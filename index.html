DevOps Benifit What It Does? Benefit To You
CICD Growth Change your app without it having to go down for maintenance Make updates & add features instantly while your app is still up and available for customers
Version Control Growth Save and use multiple versions of your app Show different versions of your app to different target markets
Monitoring & Logging Cost Saving & Growth Monitor and log your apps data and customers behavior Reveal ways to reduces costs and ways to grow your user base
Containerization Scaling Package your app in a portable container that can run on any operating system (iOS, Windows or Linux) Grow your user/customer base by empowering your app to run on any operating system a user may have

Cloud Engineering Benifit What It Does? Benefit To You
Migration Cost Saving & Growth Move your app from one cloud platform to another one Save money by running your app on affordable cloud platforms like Digital Ocean or grow your app by running on a multi-service platform like AWS
Storage Scaling & Growth Store audio, video or any kind of file your app uses in the cloud Keep your apps files readily available and accessible from anywhere by storing securley them in the cloud
PaaS Scaling & Growth Allow your cloud platform to run your app and handle the underlying services need to keep it up and running Focus on your business and your customers while your cloud provider handles all of the technical things your app requires
DBaaS Cost Saving & Growth Capture and store all your users data without managing a database Reveal insights about your users behavior that can help your save money and identify growth oppertunities
